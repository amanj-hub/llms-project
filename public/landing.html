✦ FEATURES
Everything you need to manage knowledge
A powerful toolkit for capturing lessons, organizing documents, and sharing insights across your team.
📁

Folder-Based Organization

Create nested folders to organize lessons and projects — just like Google Drive. Keep your knowledge structured and easy to navigate.

📎

Multi-File Uploads

Attach PDFs, Word docs, and PowerPoint files to any lesson. Drag-and-drop up to 5 files per entry with automatic type detection.

🔐

Public & Private Sharing

Control who sees your content. Share publicly with the community or keep sensitive lessons private to your account only.

🔍

Smart Search & Tags

Find any lesson instantly with full-text search across projects, technologies, and tags. Filter by type, impact, and category.

🔑

Google & Email Auth

Sign up in seconds with Google OAuth or traditional email. Secure JWT-based sessions keep your data protected.

📊

Analytics Dashboard

Track trending lessons, technology popularity, and team activity. AI-powered recommendations surface relevant knowledge.
